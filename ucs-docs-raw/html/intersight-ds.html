Cisco Intersight simplifies IT operations for the AI era, providing unified, intelligent management of all Cisco UCS® compute infrastructure from core to edge.

Product overview

Cisco Intersight shifts IT operations from reactive to proactive, addressing the significant challenges faced by IT operations teams and server administrators who struggle with increasing complexity, distributed environments, and the demands of both traditional and emerging AI workloads. Intersight works by leveraging a cloud-native, API-first architecture to deliver policy-based lifecycle management, automation, and unparalleled visibility across data centers, colocation facilities, and distributed edge locations.

Customers, particularly enterprises and organizations with extensive and geographically dispersed IT infrastructure, use Intersight to simplify management at scale. It consolidates disparate tools; automates complex tasks like provisioning, OS installation, firmware updates, and VLAN changes; and integrates seamlessly with Cisco® networking and security solutions for a holistic view. The primary business benefits include accelerating AI initiatives, strengthening security and compliance, and significantly improving operational efficiency and agility, thereby freeing up valuable IT resources to drive strategic business value.

Unified, holistic management across your entire IT estate

Cisco Intersight uniquely delivers a single, consistent experience for managing compute infrastructure, supporting both traditional and emerging AI workloads. The unified platform spans data centers, colocation facilities, and dispersed edge environments, providing comprehensive visibility and control from one centralized location. Unlike other vendors that often require multiple tools or offer limited functionality, Intersight manages the broadest range of Cisco UCS server form factors and generations. This holistic approach seamlessly integrates with Cisco networking and security solutions, offering a complete view and control over your entire IT estate.

Intersight stands apart with its unparalleled deployment flexibility—available as SaaS, a connected virtual appliance, or a private air-gapped option—and its API-first architecture. This design simplifies operations, accelerates automation initiatives, and ensures consistent policy enforcement and control across your entire server fleet, empowering IT teams to drive strategic business value rather than “firefighting.”

Licensing

Intersight is licensed on a subscription basis with a choice of license editions. Capabilities increase with the different license tiers. Customers can purchase a subscription duration of one, three, or five years and choose the Cisco UCS server volume tier they need for the selected subscription duration.

Starting from the M7 server generation, an Intersight Essentials or above license is required. For earlier server generations, an Intersight license is highly recommended, but not required.

Customers can purchase an Essentials or above license when they purchase the server using the Cisco ordering tool. See Table 6 for ordering PIDs.

Table 4.           Cisco Intersight licensing tiers

License tier

Description

Essentials

Includes functionality for server lifecycle management, for example:

●  Platform-level settings for user and SDK/API access, permissions, licensing, and audit. Support for health monitoring, tagging and search, basic data inventory, and multifactor authentication.
●  Support for Cisco UCS basic inventory, firmware, and dashboard management
●  Simplification of compute infrastructure management with policy-based configurations using server profiles for Cisco UCS, converged, and hyperconverged infrastructure
●  Monitor and track telemetry information (data collection every 10 minutes and 90 days of historical data)
●  Custom explorations with Intersight Metrics Explorer
●  Cisco Intersight Add-on for Splunk: Monitor your Cisco UCS infrastructure from Splunk
●  Topology views to swiftly identify, diagnose, and resolve physical cabling issues across your Cisco UCS domain
●  Power policy management for servers, BIOS, and OS as well as dynamic power rebalancing
●  Support for Cisco Compute Hyperconverged with Nutanix
●  Receiving alerts about endpoint devices that are impacted by supported security advisories and field notices along with recommended remediation
●  Acceleration of troubleshooting with hardware compatibility alerts, proactive RMAs, and automated gathering and uploading of log files for Cisco Intersight–connected devices through the Cisco Technical Assistance Center (Cisco TAC)

Note: Detailed feature list can be found here: Intersight.com Licensing Page.

Advantage

Offers all the functionality of Essentials, plus features for automation, orchestration, and advanced server management, monitoring, and metrics. For example:

●  Create and execute complex workflows with a drag-and-drop designer or incorporate existing automations from Ansible, Terraform, or other tools using the Cisco Intersight API and SDKs
●  Simplify the cloud experience with visibility of virtualization infrastructure and normalization of operations across multiple clouds
●  Monitor and track telemetry information with enriched topology views that provide error and environmental metrics and network bandwidth/utilization along with data collection every minute and two years of historical data
●  Diagnose device-level issues using error metrics and view detailed information for physical connection links
●  Use FlexPod converged infrastructure inventory, including servers, networking, storage, virtualization, and Cisco fabric interconnects
●  Use third-party storage support: view inventory and alarms of storage arrays and automate day-to-day operations using supported storage tasks
●  Resolve issues faster by ingesting data from Cisco Intersight–connected servers, storage, data-center networking, and virtual machines into the ServiceNow IT operations management platform using the ServiceNow Service Graph Connector for Cisco Intersight plugin and by raising incidents in ServiceNow for alarms and advisories from Cisco Intersight using the Cisco Intersight Incident Management Integration plugin, available on the ServiceNow store
●  Manage Cisco Unified Edge as a fleet (a logical grouping of resources) for simplified, scalable administration. Workloads, leveraging blueprints curated by Cisco and user-defined parameters, apply “golden configurations” fleet-wide, enabling centralized automation and consistent configuration across all resources.

Note: A detailed feature list can be found here: Intersight.com Licensing Page.

Ordering information

Intersight subscriptions can be ordered for periods ranging from one to five years, with any increments in between. In addition, discounts are available for longer terms, and volume discounts are available for customers ordering more than 200 device (server) subscriptions at the same time. Separate license options are detailed in Table 4 for the SaaS version on Intersight.com and the virtual appliance.

Cisco Smart Accounts and Smart Licensing are mandatory for Intersight. In addition, a Cisco user account is mandatory to use the Intersight user interface.
